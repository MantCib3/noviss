How It Works

You receive a structured PDF report. The balance is due on delivery.

About Us

NOVISS is an Australian-based service focused on analysing publicly available digital behaviour. The aim is to turn scattered online signals into clear, structured insights.

The work is grounded in disciplined, evidence-based analysis. Findings are presented with explicit confidence levels, and limitations are clearly stated. Probabilistic links are treated as such, never as certainties.

The objective is simple: reduce ambiguity and provide information that is coherent, reliable, and usable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do you use custom quotes instead of fixed pricing?

The level of effort varies depending on the quality of the starting information and the commonality of the username. Our free preliminary review ensures you receive a fair and accurate quote based on the actual work required.

What if little or no public information is found?

The preliminary review will clearly indicate if the public footprint is too limited to produce meaningful results. In such cases, we will advise against proceeding.

Is the deposit refundable?

Yes. If we are unable to deliver meaningful findings after commencing the investigation, the deposit will be refunded in full.

How do you protect client privacy?

All client inquiries and subject details are handled with strict confidentiality. Reports are delivered securely, and all client-related data is permanently deleted after project completion.

Can I request focus on specific platforms?

Absolutely. Please note any preferred platforms or particular areas of interest in your intake form.

Which platforms do you search?

We conduct searches across major social media platforms, gaming networks (including Steam, Discord, Roblox, Twitch, Epic Games, and Battle.net), forums, Reddit, and relevant niche communities.
