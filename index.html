How It Works

We begin with a short intake where you provide the primary username or handle, plus any supporting details such as a photo, email, or particular areas you want examined.

We then conduct a free preliminary review of publicly available sources and send you a custom quote detailing the expected scope and effort. There is no obligation at this stage.

If the quote is acceptable, you pay a 50% deposit to proceed. All payments are processed securely.

Once authorised, we carry out detailed cross-referencing across platforms, examine observable public activity and patterns, and include limited ethical verification only where it materially strengthens the findings.

On completion you receive a clear, structured PDF report. The balance is payable on delivery.

Our Approach

NOVISS is committed to delivering precise, ethical, and thoroughly cross-referenced OSINT. We focus on subtle but meaningful public clues, maintain strict ethical standards, and apply natural engagement only when it materially improves the reliability of findings. Our goal is to provide clients with clear, trustworthy insights — never rushed or speculative conclusions. All work is conducted exclusively within legal and ethical boundaries using publicly available sources.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do you use custom quotes instead of fixed pricing?

The level of effort varies significantly depending on the quality of the starting information and the commonality of the username. Our free preliminary review ensures you receive a fair and accurate quote based on the actual work required.

What if little or no public information is found?

The preliminary review will clearly indicate if the public footprint is too limited to produce meaningful results. In such cases, we will advise against proceeding.

Is the deposit refundable?

Yes. If we are unable to deliver meaningful public information after commencing the investigation, the deposit will be refunded in full.

How do you protect client privacy?

All client inquiries and subject details are handled with strict confidentiality. Reports are delivered securely, and all client-related data is permanently deleted after project completion.

Can I request focus on specific platforms?

Absolutely. Please note any preferred platforms or particular areas of interest in your intake form.

Which platforms do you search?

We conduct searches across major social media platforms, gaming networks (including Steam, Discord, Roblox, Twitch, Epic Games, and Battle.net), forums, Reddit, and relevant niche communities.

Disclaimers

  • All investigations are strictly limited to publicly available information and minimal ethical engagement.
  • Reports contain only non-private, inferred insights. We never provide raw private messages, restricted data, or direct contact information.
  • Results are not guaranteed and depend entirely on what is publicly visible at the time of the investigation.
  • This service is not intended for use in legal proceedings, court evidence, debt collection, or any official investigative purposes. For such requirements, please engage a licensed Queensland private investigator.
  • NOVISS fully complies with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988. Client information is kept to a minimum and securely deleted after report delivery.
